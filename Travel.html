Travel Information

Flights

Edinburgh has a well connected international airport. The city centre can be reached by a 10-20 minute ride by tram, bus or by taxi.

Train

The closest train station to the conference venue is Waverley Station.
This is roughly a 30 minute walk to the conference centre, or can be easily reached by bus.

Coach or bus

Edinburgh is easily accessible from around the UK by National Express and Megabus services. The central bus station is in the city centre, and is about a 10 minute drive, or 20 minute bus journey from the conference venue.

Car

Travel by car is not recommended, as parking in Edinburgh is typically very expensive, and is not available at the conference venue as standard.
Please feel free to contact us for additional help and information if you require parking for acessibility needs.
