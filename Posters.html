Poster Information

Abstract Selection

We will be selecting 164 abstracts for poster presentations, 5 abstracts for oral presentations, and 10 abstracts for flash talks. You will be informed whether your abstract was selected for a poster or oral presentation by 14th February at the latest.

Poster Sessions

Presenters will be assigned one of two groups - the first group will display and present their posters in Poster Sessions I-II, and the second group will present in Poster Sessions IV-V. Poster change-overs will happen during Session III.

You will be told which poster sessions you will be presenting at by e-mail ahead of the meeting, but this information will also be held at the registration desk.

Posters can be stored in Boardroom 2 (see floorplan) during the changeover day.

Poster Dimensions

Posters can be up to size A0 (33.1 x 46.8 inches), and must be in PORTRAIT orientation
