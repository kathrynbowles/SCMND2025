About SCMND 2025

Our goal is to host a vibrant, inclusive symposium for networking, outreach and education in the use of stem cells to model neurodegeneration

As researchers using stem cell models to study the biology and treatment of neurodegenerative diseases, we found that there was currently no forum in this field in which to discuss and compare our work, approaches, or to facilitate collaboration

We envision this symposium will be an exploration of the successes and ongoing studies in the field, and an opportunity for the community internationally to come together, make connections and discuss their work.

We have aimed to put together an agenda that represents the wealth of research in stem cell modelling internationally. In doing so, we have ensured a gender-balanced agenda, with speakers from diverse geographical backgrounds, and provided a platform for early career researchers to share their work and participate in discussion.

SCMND 2025 is hosted by the UK DRI as part of the Cure-ND network, with support from additional sponsors.
It will return in 2027!
